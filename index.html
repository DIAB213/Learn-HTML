Types of Fish :

Freshwater Fish

Freshwater fish are those that spend some or all of their lives in fresh water,
such as rivers and lakes, with a salinity of less than 1.05%, we sell all kinds of freshwater fish.

Saltwater Fish

Saltwater fish, also called marine fish, are fish that live in ocean water.
Saltwater fish can swim and live alone or live in a large group together, called a school of fish.
Saltwater fish are very popular among deep sea fishermen and aquariums all over the country.
